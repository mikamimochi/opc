Top 5 Colors

5. Baby Blue

Baby blue is such a pleasing color. It reminds me of cool ocean breeze blowing through the window. Dreamy clouds.

4. Petal Pink

Petal pink reminds me of a moment in the Cherry Blossom Festival. The soft petals being carried away by the wind.

3. Brilliant White

Something about the white makes me think of the daytime moon. It also feels as if the clear blue sky is reflected on icy snow.

2. Pink Marshmallow

Marshmallows and cotton candies are my favorite. Pink is my favorite. Pink marshmallow is the two mixed, literal perfection.

1. Lavender

Lavender is my favorite color. My mom always told me her love for lavenders. Whenever I see this color, I always think of my mom. This is love.
