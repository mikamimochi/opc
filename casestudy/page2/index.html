P R O J E C T

This is a project based from Cecilia Song's airtable collection of aquarium fishes. The website highlights the various temperature level the fishes live under.

The website has a playful, positive, and educational feel to it. Inspired from childhood educational websites full of welcoming and easy-to-use graphics and features.

There is a temperature bar to show the unique idea of the airtable. The users get a grasp of the temperature these fishes thrive under.
